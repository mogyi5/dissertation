About

This is an MSc project completed in the summer of 2019, in accordance with the rules of the School of Computing at the University of Glasgow. The primary aim of this project is to try to eliminate unnecessary data duplication between the patients' and health professionals' data. The app aims to simplify interactions between patients and staff, as well as interactons between the front and back ends.
MyHealthDB was written in Python using the Django framework. The libraries have made it much easier to work with and create.
